Why Choose SimpleVote?

We've combined the best democratic principles with modern technology to create the perfect voting experience for small groups.

Time-Bound Voting

Set exact start and end times for your polls. Automatic closing ensures fair and timely results.

One Vote Per Person

Strict enforcement of one vote per person using session tracking. No duplicate voting, ever.

Invisible Results

Results stay hidden until the poll closes, preventing bandwagon effects and ensuring unbiased voting.

Beautiful Analytics

When voting ends, results are revealed with stunning visualizations and detailed breakdowns.

About SimpleVote

We believe democracy should be simple, accessible, and secure for everyone.

Our Mission

SimpleVote was born from a simple observation: voting shouldn't be complicated. Whether you're deciding on a classroom activity, team lunch, or community project, the process should be transparent, fair, and easy to understand.

We've eliminated the complexity while maintaining the integrity of the democratic process. No complex setups, no confusing interfaces - just pure, simple voting that works.

Security First

Your votes and data are protected with enterprise-grade security.

Accessibility

Designed for everyone, regardless of technical expertise.

Fairness

One vote per person, anonymous results, and transparent processes.
